Ooty - Your Travel Roadmap

Explore Ooty

Ooty, the "Queen of Hill Stations," offers a scenic escape with its lush green tea gardens, colonial charm, and breathtaking views.

  • Ooty Lake: Enjoy boating or a serene walk around the beautiful lake.
  • Doddabetta Peak: The highest point in Ooty offering panoramic views of the Nilgiri hills.
  • Botanical Gardens: A must-visit for nature lovers, showcasing a wide variety of flora.
  • Tea Gardens: Visit the lush tea estates and learn about the tea-making process.
