Pondicherry Roadmap

Explore the French Colonial Charm

Pondicherry offers a perfect blend of French colonial heritage, pristine beaches, and cultural experiences. Here's your itinerary for the trip:

  • Day 1: Arrival at Pondicherry, Explore the French Quarter
  • Day 1: Visit Sri Aurobindo Ashram
  • Day 2: Enjoy the Promenade Beach and the Gandhi Statue
  • Day 2: Visit Auroville, the International Township
  • Day 3: Explore the Chunnambar Boat House and Paradise Beach
  • Day 3: Departure

Pondicherry is known for its serene atmosphere, vibrant street art, and stunning coastline!
