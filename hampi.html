Hampi - Your Travel Roadmap

Explore Hampi

Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage site, is known for its ancient ruins, stunning temples, and breathtaking landscapes.

  • Vittala Temple: A beautiful temple famous for its stone chariot and musical pillars.
  • Virupaksha Temple: One of the oldest temples in Hampi, dedicated to Lord Shiva.
  • Hampi Bazaar: A historic market area with ruins and ancient arches.
  • Matanga Hill: The best spot for panoramic views of the entire Hampi region.
