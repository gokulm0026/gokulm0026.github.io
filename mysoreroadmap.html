Mysore Roadmap

Experience Royalty at Mysore

Mysore is a city of heritage, culture, and majestic architecture. From the royal Mysore Palace to the nearby Chamundi Hill, here's your itinerary:

  • Day 1: Arrival at Mysore, Visit the Mysore Palace
  • Day 1: Explore St. Philomena's Church
  • Day 2: Visit Chamundi Hill and the Chamundeshwari Temple
  • Day 2: Explore the Mysore Zoo and Karanji Lake
  • Day 3: Visit the Ranganathittu Bird Sanctuary
  • Day 3: Departure

Mysore offers royal palaces, scenic views, and a glimpse of traditional South Indian culture!
