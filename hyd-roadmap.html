Roadmap for Hyderabad

Explore the rich history, culture, and landmarks of Hyderabad. Here's the list of places you'll visit:

  • Charminar: A historic mosque with four grand arches, an iconic symbol of Hyderabad.
  • Golconda Fort: A grand fort with a fascinating history and panoramic views of the city.
  • Ramoji Film City: A massive film studio complex with exciting attractions and sets.
  • Hussain Sagar Lake: A large lake offering boat rides and views of the city skyline.
  • Qutb Shahi Tombs: Historic tombs of the Qutb Shahi dynasty kings.
