Kerala Backwaters - Your Travel Roadmap

Explore the Kerala Backwaters

Experience the tranquil beauty of Kerala's backwaters, where lush greenery, serene waters, and houseboats await you. Here's your itinerary for an unforgettable journey.

  • Alleppey: Famous for its houseboats and picturesque backwaters.
  • Kumarakom: Known for its bird sanctuary and peaceful lake views.
  • Vembanad Lake: One of the largest lakes in Kerala, offering a peaceful houseboat experience.
  • Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary: A haven for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts.
