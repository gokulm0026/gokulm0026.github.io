Mahabalipuram Roadmap

Discover the Ancient Sculptures and Shore Temples

Mahabalipuram is a UNESCO World Heritage site, known for its stunning rock-cut temples and intricate sculptures. Here's your itinerary for the trip:

  • Day 1: Arrival at Mahabalipuram, Visit the Shore Temple
  • Day 1: Explore the Five Rathas (Panch Rathas)
  • Day 2: Visit Arjuna's Penance (Descent of the Ganges)
  • Day 2: Explore the Cave Temples and Mandapams
  • Day 3: Visit the Tiger Cave and the nearby beaches
  • Day 3: Departure

Enjoy exploring Mahabalipuram's ancient architectural marvels and the stunning coastline!
